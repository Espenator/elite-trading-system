## 📊 MARKET STRUCTURE FRAMEWORK

### **Structure Classification (Our Market DNA)**

| Structure Type | Definition | Scanner Signal | Action |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **Strong Uptrend** | Clean HH + HL pattern, steep angle | LONG scan, Score >60, Fractal >80 | Enter on HL retest, stop below HL |
| **Weak Uptrend** | Shallow HH/HL, overlapping candles | LONG scan, Score 40-60 | Wait for HH break confirmation |
| **Consolidation** | Failed HH attempts, equal highs/lows | Mixed signals, low scores | WAIT - no structural edge |
| **Weak Downtrend** | Shallow LL/LH, slow descent | SHORT scan, Score 40-60 | Wait for LL break confirmation |
| **Strong Downtrend** | Clean LL + LH pattern, steep angle | SHORT scan, Score >60, Fractal >80 | Enter on LH retest, stop above LH |

### **Structural Events (Market's Only Two Moves)**

**Change of Character (CHOCH)** = Warning Shot[^7][^8]

- Uptrend: Price breaks below last HL (buyers failed to defend)
- Downtrend: Price breaks above last LH (sellers failed to defend)
- Scanner shows: Setup type changes, Fractal score spikes, Volume clusters
- Action: Reduce position size, prepare for reversal, tighten stops

**Confirmation of Trend (COT)** = Green Light[^5][^8]

- Uptrend: New HH made with strong volume (buyers still in control)
- Downtrend: New LL made with strong volume (sellers still in control)
- Scanner shows: High composite score, Fractal 80-100, Vol >50
- Action: Enter with full position, structural stop placement, ride the trend


### **Clean Zones (Institutional Footprints)**[^6][^9]

Clean zones are NOT lines—they are **areas where large orders were filled** before a major structural move:

- **Supply Zone:** Area before a strong LL/LH move down (institutional distribution)
- **Demand Zone:** Area before a strong HH/HL move up (institutional accumulation)
- **Scanner Translation:** Setup types show these as ROLLOVER_MEAN_FADE (supply) or ROLLOVER_MEAN_REVERSION (demand)

**How to identify on chart:**

1. Find the last candle(s) before a massive structural break
2. Box the body + wicks of that consolidation area
3. That zone = where institutions last committed
4. Price returning to zone = retest of institutional intent

| **Composite Score (final)** | Overall structural quality | Score >60 = high-probability structure, 40-60 = marginal, <40 = avoid |

## 📐 THE RULE OF THREE (Structural Confirmation Filter)[^10][^4]

**NEVER enter a trade unless ALL THREE align:**

### **1. STRUCTURE (Primary Filter)**

✅ **For LONG:** Must show clean HH + HL pattern on entry timeframe, aligned with higher timeframe uptrend
✅ **For SHORT:** Must show clean LL + LH pattern on entry timeframe, aligned with higher timeframe downtrend
❌ **If consolidating:** Structure is ambiguous → WAIT

### **2. VOLUME (Conviction Filter)**[^4][^5]

✅ **At structural breaks:** Volume must be >1.5x average (institutional commitment)
✅ **At clean zones:** High volume rejection = zone defended by big money
❌ **Low volume break:** Suspicious; likely fakeout → WAIT

### **3. TIMEFRAME ALIGNMENT (Probability Filter)**[^11][^10]

| Your Entry TF | Higher TF Structure Required | Logic |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| 5-min entry | 15-min + 1-hour must show same trend direction | Don't fight the bigger auction |
| 15-min entry | 1-hour + 4-hour must show same trend direction | Lower TF is just entry precision |
| 1-hour entry | 4-hour + Daily must show same trend direction | Higher TF = directional boss |

**CRITICAL:** If higher timeframe structure conflicts with scanner signal → **DO NOT TRADE**

### **STEP 1: Higher Timeframe Structure Mapping (4-Hour \& Daily)**[^8][^5]

**Before opening any position, answer these questions:**

**QQQ \& SPY Structure Check:**

- Scanner says STRONG_BULL → Validate: QQQ/SPY both showing clean HH/HL pattern
- Scanner says WEAK_BULL → Validate: QQQ/SPY making shallow HH/HL or consolidating
- Scanner says BEAR → Validate: QQQ/SPY showing LL/LH pattern

**VIX Structure Check:**

- VIX <15: Low fear, favor LONG structural setups, institutions buying dips
- VIX 15-20: Normal range, trade best structural setups both directions
- VIX 20-25: Elevated, reduce size, favor SHORT structural setups
- VIX >25: High fear, wait for CHOCH before reversing to LONG

| Clean zone proximity | Near demand zone or broke above supply zone | Near supply zone or broke below demand zone |
| Volume at last swing | Volume spike at last HL formation | Volume spike at last LH formation |

**If ANY box unchecked → Move to next candidate**

### **STEP 3: Entry Precision Using Structure**[^13][^10]

**Structural Entry Rules:**

**For LONG (HH/HL structure confirmed):**

1. Wait for price to pull back to last HL or nearby demand zone
2. Watch for rejection candle (bullish engulfing, hammer, strong close near high)
3. Enter on break above that rejection candle's high
4. Stop goes just below the HL or demand zone low (thesis invalidation point)
5. Target = next structural level (prior HH, supply zone, or measured move)

**For SHORT (LL/LH structure confirmed):**

1. Wait for price to rally to last LH or nearby supply zone
2. Watch for rejection candle (bearish engulfing, shooting star, strong close near low)
3. Enter on break below that rejection candle's low
4. Stop goes just above the LH or supply zone high (thesis invalidation point)
5. Target = next structural level (prior LL, demand zone, or measured move)

**Position Sizing by Structure Quality:**

- Fractal 80-100 + Vol 80-100 + timeframe aligned = Full size (5% portfolio)
- Fractal 60-79 or Vol 50-79 = Half size (2.5% portfolio)
- Fractal <60 or Vol <50 = Do not trade (structure too weak)

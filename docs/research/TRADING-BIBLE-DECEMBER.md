### **9:20 AM - POSITION SIZING \& ORDER PREPARATION**

**Duration: 5-10 minutes**

**For each APPROVED trade:**

1. **Calculate Position Size (Van Tharp Method)**

**Formula:**

```
Risk Amount = Account Size × Risk % (from regime)
Risk Per Share = Entry Price - Stop Price
Position Size = Risk Amount ÷ Risk Per Share
```

**Example:**

```
Account: $800,000
Regime: GREEN → Risk % = 2.0%
Risk Amount = $800,000 × 0.02 = $16,000

Entry: $150.00
Stop: $145.00 (structural invalidation point)
Risk Per Share = $150 - $145 = $5.00

Position Size = $16,000 ÷ $5.00 = 3,200 shares
Total Capital = 3,200 × $150 = $480,000 (60% of account)
```

2. **Scale-In Plan (33-33-34 Rule)**

Never enter full position at once. Always scale in:

```
Entry 1 (33%): 1,056 shares @ $150.00 [9:30-10:00 AM window]
Entry 2 (33%): 1,056 shares @ $151.50 [If dips to HL retest]
Entry 3 (34%): 1,088 shares @ $153.00 [If breaks above entry 2]
```

**Timing:**
    - Entry 1: Market open confirmation (9:30-10:00 AM)
    - Entry 2: Pullback to structural support (watch for retest)
    - Entry 3: Momentum acceleration (volume surge)
3. **Stop Loss Management**

**Initial Stop:**
    - Place at structural invalidation point
    - For LONG: Below last HL or demand zone (typically 2.5x ATR)
    - For SHORT: Above last LH or supply zone (typically 2.5x ATR)
    - Example: \$145.00 (3.3% below entry)

**Breakeven Stop:**
    - Once position up 3% (1R achieved), move stop to entry price
    - Example: When price hits \$154.50, move stop to \$150.00

**Trailing Stop:**
    - After breakeven, trail 50% of profit
    - Example: Price at \$160, stop at \$155 (trailing \$5 below)
    - Or use ATR trailing: Trail 1.5x ATR below highest high
4. **Profit Targets**

**Target 1 (1R - 50% position):**
    - First structural barrier (resistance for LONG, support for SHORT)
    - Example: \$155.00 (next resistance level, 3.3% gain)
    - Action: Take profit on 50% (1,600 shares), move stop to breakeven

**Target 2 (2R - 50% position):**
    - Second structural barrier or next major level
    - Example: \$160.00 (major resistance, 6.7% gain)
    - Action: Take profit on remaining 50% (1,600 shares)

**Target 3 (3R+ - If 2R Hit):**
    - Trail remaining position with ATR stop
    - Let winners run until structural invalidation
5. **Record in Google Sheets - "Live Signals" Tab**

Update row for this trade:

    - Column B: Symbol
    - Column C: Direction (LONG/SHORT)
    - Column D: Setup Type
    - Column E: Entry Price
    - Column F: Stop Loss
    - Column G: Target 1
    - Column H: Target 2

    - Column J: Risk Amount (\$)
    - Column K: Expected R:R (2:1 minimum)
    - Column L: Status (PENDING)

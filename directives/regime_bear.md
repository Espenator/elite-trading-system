# Bear Market Directives

## Entry Rules
- Favor mean-reversion entries at support levels
- Short rallies into resistance with volume divergence
- Tighter stops: use ATR x 1.5 for stop placement
- Require extra confirmation: 2+ indicators aligned

## Position Sizing
- Position scale multiplier: 0.6x normal
- Reduce max portfolio heat to 4%
- Reduce max single position to 1.5%
- Smaller position sizes across the board

## Exit Rules
- Take profits quickly — don't hold for extended moves
- Fixed targets preferred over trailing stops
- Exit at 1.5R for longs, 2R for shorts

## Bias
- Default bias: DEFENSIVE
- Longs only on extreme oversold bounces
- Cash is a position — no shame in sitting out
- Mean-reversion > momentum in bear regimes
